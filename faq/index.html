FAQ

Frequently asked questions about My Own Fable.

Are the stories original?

Yes. The site is built around original fables and original supporting copy rather than scraped or copied collections.

Who is the site for?

Families, teachers, students, and general readers who enjoy short moral stories and reflective discussion.

Can I reuse the stories?

Please contact us for permissions beyond brief quotations with attribution.

Does the site use third-party services?

The site may use trusted third-party services for hosting, security, measurement, or advertising. Privacy and cookie details are explained in the policy pages.
