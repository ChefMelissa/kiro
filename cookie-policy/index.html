Cookie Policy

Detailed information about cookies, advertising cookies, consent choices, and browser controls.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small files stored on your device. They help websites remember choices, measure use, protect security, and support advertising where permitted.

2. Cookie categories

  • Essential cookies: needed for basic site operation and security.
  • Preference cookies: remember choices such as privacy settings or display preferences.
  • Analytics cookies: help understand how visitors use pages and where improvements are needed.
  • Advertising cookies: may be used by Google and other partners to serve, limit, personalize, and measure advertising.

3. Personalized advertising

Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to personalize advertising where allowed. You can control personalized advertising through Google Ads Settings or relevant industry opt-out tools.

4. EEA and UK consent

Visitors in the EEA or UK may be asked for consent before non-essential cookies are used. You may be able to accept, reject, or manage cookie categories through a privacy message.

5. Managing cookies

You can delete or block cookies through your browser. Doing so may reduce personalization or disable some site features.

6. Contact

Questions about cookies can be sent to hello@myownfable.com.
