Editorial Policy

How My Own Fable approaches originality, story quality, corrections, and reader trust.

1. Mission

My Own Fable publishes original stories that help readers think about character, choices, and everyday wisdom. The goal is to create a useful reading library, not a collection of copied summaries.

2. Originality

We aim to publish original fables, original morals, and original supporting materials. Public-domain inspiration may inform the tradition of fable writing, but pages should not copy another author's protected expression.

3. Quality process

Stories are checked for readability, tone, family suitability, structure, clear moral value, and practical reflection. We prefer useful, thoughtful pages over thin filler content.

4. Corrections

If you find a mistake, unclear wording, or concern about a page, contact us with the page URL and details. We may revise pages to improve accuracy, clarity, and usefulness.

5. Reader safety

We avoid graphic, hateful, or adult-oriented material. The site is intended to stay gentle, accessible, and appropriate for broad family reading.
