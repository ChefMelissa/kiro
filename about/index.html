About My Own Fable

Learn about the purpose, style, and reading experience behind My Own Fable.

My Own Fable is a colorful reading library of original fables and moral stories. It is designed for families, classrooms, and readers who enjoy gentle stories with clear topics.

Each fable is written to stand on its own, with a moral, reflection questions, and key takeaways that help readers connect the story to everyday life.

The site is organized by topics such as courage, kindness, honesty, patience, wisdom, community, and friendship, so readers can choose the kind of story they need.
