Privacy Rights & GDPR Requests

Information about privacy requests, GDPR-style rights, verification, and contact steps.

1. Privacy requests

You may contact us about privacy rights requests, including access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, portability, or withdrawal of consent where applicable.

2. How to contact us

Email hello@myownfable.com with the subject line “Privacy Request.” Include the nature of the request and enough information for us to understand it.

3. Verification

We may need to verify identity before acting on a request so that we do not disclose or delete information improperly.

4. Response timing

We aim to respond within a reasonable period and within legally required timelines where they apply.

5. Complaints

If you are in a region with a data protection authority, you may have the right to complain to that authority if you believe your data rights have not been handled properly.
